Next Optimized Generation of Autonomous Suburbs

With the adoption of distributed, on-demand autonomous mobility services, many of the standard land use and development patterns that have typified suburban form since the early 20th century will become redundant or obsolete within the next twenty years.

These disruptions will present significant opportunities for planners and designers to optimize future neighborhoods by integrating autonomous mobility.

This website will showcase ongoing research and provide access to innovative digital design tools.
