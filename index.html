NOGAS

Next Optimized Generation of Autonomous Suburbs

With the adoption of distributed, on-demand autonomous mobility services, many of the standardized land use and development patterns that have typified suburban form since the early 20th century have become redundant or will be obsolete within the next twenty yeras.

These distruptions will present significant opportunities for optimizing the suburban environment to address several distinct challenges.

This website will showcase ongoing research and provides access to innovative tools for planners and designers working in suburban communities.


The website will be launched in Nov. 2022

as a collaborative effort between the P-REX Lab, MIT Center for Advanced Urbanism and Toyota Mobility Foundation. Please check their websites for preliminary information about this project.
